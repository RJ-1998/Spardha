FEBRUARY 23-25

Sabrang is a annual Tech-Management-Cultural festival of JK LAKSHMIPAT UNIVERSITY Jaipur where students, artists and performers showcase their talent and skills in the varoius events. Here at Sabrang, you become a part of a ride which will lead you to a destination where you discover the lights of happiness and colors of joy.

Take a leap with us and feel the heartbeats dancing on invicible music. Join us and get a bundle full of unforgettable memories and experience at Sabrang starting from 26th October-28th October 2018!
